Q&A

- Why did you decide to work on this topic, what's your interest there?

When I think about the natural system, I want to create something familiar to people and also interesting to see, that's how I come across the idea of honey gathering.

- How did you envision your system in the beginning and how did that vision change?

At the beginning the idea is simpler, only using the dot and circle to represent bees and flowers, later on I find out I can use gif to create get more interesting results.

- How did you deal with the complexity in your system?

Create different classes

- How did you decide on the interactive elements?

Using the mouse to add and remove flowers would be the most direct way to interact with this prototype, and by clicking the screen you can have the idea of planting a flower

- What would be some paths for future development of the project?

  • there are could be more bees on the screen at some time
  • the flower could be prettier with more attributes
